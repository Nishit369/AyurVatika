About the 3D Virtual Garden

Welcome to your virtual 3D Garden.

Here, you can navigate through a variety of wonderful plants and trees and explore their benefits.

You’ll Find :

Interactive 3D Models: Immersive experience as you can look from every direction and understand the structure and texture of the plant.

Comprehensive Details: Dive deep into each plant’s botanical secrets, medicinal uses, and cultivation tips.

Feel free to explore and engage with the flora. The garden is best experienced in full-screen mode.

For optimal navigation, use the arrow keys or the W, A, S, and D keys. You can also use your mouse to change the viewing direction.

Additional information about the plants will appear on your screen as you approach them.

Controls

W/A/S/D: Move around

Mouse: Look around

C: Crouch

Space: Toggle pointer lock

M: Show Menu

Enter: Start exploration

Esc: Stop exploration

⬆: Move up the stairs

J: Move down the stairs
